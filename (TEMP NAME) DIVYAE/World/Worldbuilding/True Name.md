A **true name** is a creature's hidden name that, rather than just acting as a label, metaphysically truly belongs to that creature. All beings have true names. 

At a time long ago, no true names were known. When humans began to connect with [[Deities]], they were able to learn their own true name. With their name came strong but uncontrollable power. Most humans perished if they tried using their true name as their source of power.

>[!quote|author] deity
>"Knowing a creature's true name can grant power over it. When using your own true name as a source of power, you must be incredibly careful, as it pulls from your own soul. It is better to take power from another holy being, so as to not harm yourself"

The deity refused to give their true name when asked.

Before this quote, people would use their true name as their name in everyday conversation. However, not too long after hearing this, they found out they could use others names as sources of power.
